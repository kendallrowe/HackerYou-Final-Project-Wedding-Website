Our Story

Victoria and I met working at a summer camp for children with neurological differences called Camp Winston.

After a few years of being friends, I was lucky enough to work up the courage to ask Vic out... while I may not have known it at the time, this was the best decision I've ever made.

5 years later - on October 6, 2018 in Barcelona, I worked up the courage in Park Guell to ask for Vic's hand in marriage. We ask for you to join us in celebrating the beginning of a new chapter of our lives together at Ancaster Mills.

We are so incredibly excited and grateful to be inviting you to join us on December 21st as we celebrate the beginning of our married lives together!
